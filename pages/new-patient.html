Welcome to Lumina

Your First Visit, Elevated

Everything you need to know before stepping into the Lumina experience.

📋
What to Expect

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive oral health assessment, digital X-rays, and a 3D scan. We take the time to understand your goals, concerns, and dental history. No rushing, no pressure — just a thorough consultation in a private suite.

📸
Digital Smile Preview

For cosmetic consultations, Dr. Voss creates a Digital Smile Design (DSD) using photography and 3D facial mapping. You'll see your potential results before any treatment begins — and you'll refine the design together until it's exactly right.

🛡️
Insurance & Payment

We accept 50+ PPO insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and Guardian. Our billing team verifies your benefits before your appointment. No insurance? We offer membership plans starting at $699/year and 0% financing through CareCredit and Sunbit.

📝
What to Bring

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and a list of current medications. If you have dental records or X-rays from a previous provider, we're happy to request them on your behalf — just let our coordinator know.

🅿️
Parking & Arrival

Complimentary valet parking is available at 414 N Camden Drive. Please arrive 10 minutes before your appointment. Our patient coordinator will greet you and guide you to your private suite.
