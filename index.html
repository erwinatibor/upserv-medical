FAQ

Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Lumina.

Still have questions?

Lumina is a concierge-level practice. We cap our patient roster to ensure unhurried appointments, offer same-day emergency access, work with a master ceramist lab for all restorations, and have a dedicated patient coordinator who manages every aspect of your care.

We begin with a digital scan and photographic analysis. Dr. Voss creates a digital smile design using DSD software — you see your results before any treatment begins. We refine the design together, then build a phased treatment plan that fits your timeline and budget.

Yes. We offer nitrous oxide, oral conscious sedation, and full IV sedation with board-certified anesthesiology support. Dr. Levy has performed over 1,200 sedation cases. We cater to highly anxious patients and complex multi-procedure days.

High-quality porcelain veneers typically last 15–25 years with proper care. We use only master-ceramist-crafted restorations in IPS e.max or zirconia-based materials. All veneers are covered by our 5-year cosmetic warranty.

We offer 0% financing through CareCredit (12–24 months) and Sunbit (up to 60 months). Our patient coordinator walks through your options before treatment begins. We also work with FSA/HSA accounts.

Many of our patients travel from across California and internationally for smile makeovers and complex rehabilitation. We coordinate with your travel schedule and accommodate intensive multi-day treatment sessions.

A straightforward veneer case takes 2–3 appointments over 3–4 weeks. Comprehensive rehabilitation with implants and orthodontics can take 12–18 months. We provide a precise timeline at your consultation.
